01 — About Us
Industry Leader.
Innovo is an industry leader in construction and innovation across the built environment, specializing in the design, engineering, construction, development, landscape and infrastructure, financing of major real estate projects across four continents. A front-runner and innovator in the built environment, Innovo's vast and varied portfolio includes more than 130 ongoing projects, encompassing luxury residential developments, villa communities, educational facilities, commercial hubs, and critical urban infrastructure.

02 — Mission & Goal
Why we build.
OUR MISSION
Pioneer in the Built Environment
To be a pioneer in construction, MEP, landscape, infrastructure and real estate development, integrating advanced technologies, encouraging diverse perspectives from our people, and implementing sustainable practices, to lead transformative change in the industry worldwide.
OUR GOAL
Transform. Drive Efficiency. Improve Outcomes.
Our goal is to transform the built environment, drive efficiency and improve outcomes for end-users. We advocate for the widespread adoption of technology through partnerships and common platforms, and work with our clients for more sustainable cities and communities that respect the environment.
